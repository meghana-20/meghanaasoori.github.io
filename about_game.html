Snake and Ladder Game Rules and Player Limitations

Objective: The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach the final square, which is usually marked as square 100.

Players:The game supports a maximum of four players. Players can choose the number of participants from 2 to 4.

Gameplay:
Players take turns to roll a six-sided die and move forward the number of squares indicated by the die roll.
If a player lands on a square at the bottom of a ladder, they will climb to the square at the top of the ladder.
If a player lands on a square with the head of a snake, they will slide down to the square at the tail of the snake.
The game continues until one player reaches the final square (100).

Winning:
The first player to land exactly on square 100 is declared the winner.
If a player's move would take them beyond square 100, they must wait until their next turn to try again.
The game ends immediately once a player reaches square 100.

Controls:Click the "Start" button and enter the number of players (2 to 4). For each player, enter their name and choose a color for their coin.
Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice and move the player's token.
Click the "Restart" button to reset the game and start over.
Click the "Exit" button to leave the game
